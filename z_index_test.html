The "Add" button below is unclickable because a transparent overlay is on top of it.

How to Debug This:

  1. Right-click the 'Add' button and choose 'Inspect'.
  2. Notice that the inspector might highlight the
    instead of the button. This is your first clue.
  3. Hover your mouse over the button in the browser. Watch which element is highlighted in the Elements panel. It will be the overlay.
  4. To confirm, select the overlay in the Elements panel, right-click it, and choose 'Hide element' or press the 'H' key.
  5. Now, try clicking the button. It should work, and you'll see a message in the console.
