Trust & Safety

Safety

Our commitment to creating a safe and responsible AI music platform.

Our Safety Principles

At Vnuno, we take safety seriously. We're committed to developing AI technology responsibly and ensuring our platform is used for creative, positive purposes. Our comprehensive safety measures protect our users and the broader community.

shield

Content Moderation

We use automated systems and human review to detect and prevent harmful content, including hate speech, explicit material, and copyright violations.

copyright

Copyright Protection

Our AI is trained to respect intellectual property. We have systems in place to prevent generation of content that infringes on existing copyrights.

verified_user

User Verification

We implement email verification and optional identity verification for creators to maintain platform integrity and prevent abuse.

report

Reporting System

Users can report concerning content or behavior. Our team reviews reports promptly and takes appropriate action.

Prohibited Uses

To maintain a safe platform, the following uses of Vnuno are strictly prohibited:

  • block Creating content that promotes hate speech, violence, or discrimination
  • block Generating explicit or sexually inappropriate content
  • block Impersonating individuals without consent
  • block Creating content intended to deceive or mislead
  • block Using the platform for illegal activities
  • block Attempting to circumvent safety filters or restrictions

Data Privacy & Security

Your Content

You retain ownership of the music you create. We don't use your private creations to train our AI models without explicit consent. Your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest.

Third-Party Sharing

We never sell your personal information. We only share data with trusted service providers necessary to operate our platform, and only under strict confidentiality agreements.

Account Security

We support two-factor authentication and recommend all users enable it. We monitor for suspicious account activity and notify you of any potential security issues.

For Parents & Guardians

Vnuno is committed to providing a safe environment for users of all ages. We recommend that children under 13 use our platform with parental supervision. Our content filters help ensure age-appropriate experiences.

Parental Controls

  • • Option to disable sharing features for underage accounts
  • • Content filtering options
  • • Activity monitoring tools
  • • Easy account deletion process

Report a Concern

If you encounter content or behavior that violates our safety guidelines, please report it immediately.
