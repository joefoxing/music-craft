Getting Started

How do I create my first song?

Simply enter a description of the song you want to create in the prompt box on the homepage, then click "Create". Our AI will generate a complete song based on your description. You can also choose from our templates for inspiration.

What types of music can I create?

Vnuno supports a wide variety of genres including pop, rock, hip-hop, electronic, classical, jazz, country, and more. You can specify the genre, mood, instruments, and style in your prompt.

Do I need musical experience?

Not at all! Vnuno is designed to be accessible to everyone, regardless of musical background. Just describe what you want, and our AI handles the technical details.

Account

How do I reset my password?

Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your email address, and we'll send you a link to reset your password. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder.

Can I change my email address?

Yes! Go to your Profile settings and click "Edit" next to your email address. You'll need to verify the new email before the change takes effect.

How do I delete my account?

We're sorry to see you go. To delete your account, go to Profile Settings > Account > Delete Account. Note that this action is permanent and will remove all your data and generated songs.

Billing

What's included in the Pro plan?

Pro subscribers get unlimited song generation, higher quality audio (320kbps), commercial usage rights, priority processing, and access to advanced editing features like vocal removal and mashup tools.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime from your Profile > Billing page. Your Pro features will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer refunds within 7 days of purchase if you haven't generated more than 5 songs. Contact support@vnuno.com for refund requests.

Troubleshooting

My song generation failed. What should I do?

First, check your internet connection. If the problem persists, try simplifying your prompt or trying a different style. You can also check our status page to see if we're experiencing any outages.

The audio won't play. How do I fix this?

Make sure your browser's audio is enabled and not muted. Try refreshing the page or using a different browser. If issues continue, clear your browser cache and cookies.
